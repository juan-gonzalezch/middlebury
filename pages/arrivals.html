ARRIVAL INFORMATION

Middlebury-School in Argentina will make a reservation under your name, in advance, for a transfer service called Taxi Ezeiza to take you from the airport to your hotel in Buenos Aires.

The company´s booth is at the Ezeiza airport: you will see their booth right after leaving the restricted zone, at the center of the main arrivals hole.The arrivals hall. The 21-mile ride takes about 45 minutes to 1 hour, and the rate is about USD $ 57 (in cash).We advise paying the service with your credit card (not in cash), since you will get a better exchange rate.

In any other case, we strongly advise taking only a registered remise (a private, hired car service) or a registered taxi from the airport, such as Vip Car. There are quite a few unscrupulous taxi drivers at the airport who take advantage of tourists unfamiliar with Buenos Aires, so it is best to stay safe and go with a registered driver that you request at a booth.
